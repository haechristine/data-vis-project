Rethinking Your Ready Meals

Final Reflection

As a college student, eating regular, nutritious meals can be difficult due to our busy schedules. The majority of us transition from getting meals from our parents to being on a meal plan, then to planning our meals. For days when I want a quick, easy meal, I want to know which store would provide the best nutritious meal. As UC San Diego students, we have both a Target and a Whole Foods that are readily accessible to us due to proximity, therefore, I decided to focus on those two stores to help determine where students should purchase their next prepared meal.
